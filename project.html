FACTS ABOUT MY DOG

This is Max, the four-legged tornado of joy that has taken over my life. With a wagging tail that could power a small city, he’s not just a pet; he’s a full-time comedian and my personal therapist.

Max is a Golden Retriever, which means he’s got the charm of a movie star and the appetite of a hungry hippo. His personality? Think of a mix between a goofy clown and a wise old sage—always ready to make you laugh or offer a comforting paw when life gets tough.

Every day with Max is an adventure filled with slobbery kisses, epic zoomies, and the occasional ‘oops, I dug up your favorite plant’ moment. He’s not just my dog; he’s my partner in crime, and together, we’re unstoppable!
